About

I later studied visual communications at Bauhaus-University Weimar and graduated with a diploma on ["Cultural Presets"](https://leanpub.com/nothingtosell-culturalpresets). In my research I conceptiulize artshows, media and internet artworks, and performances Visit www.porschuetz.de for more.
