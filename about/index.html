About

I was born and raised in East Germany. I spent a year in Utah where I made my first programming experience at an early age. I later studied visual communications at Bauhaus-University Weimar and graduated with a diploma on ["Cultural Presets"](https://leanpub.com/nothingtosell-culturalpresets). In my research I conceptiulize artshows, media and internet artworks, and performances [visit www.porschuetz.de for more](http://www.porschuetz.de).
