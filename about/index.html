About

I was born and raised in East Germany. I spent a year in Utah where I made my first programming experience at an early age. I later studied visual communications at Bauhaus-University Weimar and graduated with a diploma on "Cultural Presets". What interests me is the cognitive in-between different states or definitions that we create collectively as a culture and those that we make for ourselves - A subconscious of communication. To research this and expand my understanding of this I produce art shows, media and internet artworks, performances and events. Therefore I also dedicated my professional career to interfaces, the in between humen and machines, and or between machines amongst themselves.
